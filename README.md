Public AI flywheels are aoftware tools designed to let people to opt-in to contributing data (specifically the data about their use of AI systems, and their self-evaluation of the outputs of those systems) towards “[public AI](https://publicai.network/whitepaper/)” causes. 

For background, see the mini-book on [Data Flywheels and Public AI](https://nickmvincent.github.io/paidf_consultation/).
